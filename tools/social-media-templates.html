How It Works

From idea to published post in 5 steps

01

Brainstorm with a mind map

Use the AI Mindmap Generator to explore content angles, topics, and hooks for your social media posts.

02

Pick a template

Choose a template that matches your platform and format. Each template is sized to the exact pixel dimensions required.

03

Open in Figma

Click "Figma Template" to open a ready-to-edit design file. Customize colors, images, text, and layout in Figma.

04

Export your design

Export from Figma as PNG, JPG, or PDF at the correct resolution. Templates include safe zones and bleed guides.

05

Publish and share

Upload your finished design to your social media platform. Consistent sizing means your content always looks professional.
